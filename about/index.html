Our Story

Founded on Craftsmanship

BuildMaster was established in 2005 by a team of engineers and architects with a shared vision: to bring world-class construction standards to Lithuania. From our first small renovation project in Vilnius Old Town, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted construction firms.

Today our team of 80+ professionals handles everything from luxury residential builds to complex commercial developments, always holding our work to the highest standard.
